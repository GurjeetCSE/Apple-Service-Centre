Frequently Asked Questions

Get instant answers to common repair inquiries

How long does a typical repair take?

Most common repairs like screen and battery replacements are completed within 1-3 hours. More complex motherboard issues may take 2-4 business days.

Do you use genuine Apple parts?

Yes, we use high-quality, genuine parts for all our repairs to ensure your device functions exactly like new. All repairs come with our service guarantee.

Is there a warranty on the repairs?

Absolutely. Every repair we perform comes with a standard 90-day warranty on the parts replaced. We stand by our work!
